Important Tips and Reminders:

Helmets: are required. No excuses. The Athletics department will be inspecting each helmet.

Lessons: required for beginners. If he hasn't, Mr. Suchanek will confirm the time for lessons. In the past, lessons have taken place on Wednesdays (2pm-3pm).

Day students: If your parents are picking you up from the mountain, please communicate with one of the faculty chaperones.

Bus Rides: Ride the same vehicle to and from the mountain. Use headphones for entertainment (no speakers!). Do not leave trash on the bus.

Tardiness: A minimum of 3 infraction points will be recorded for unexcused tardiness at the mountain. If you are renting, allow enough time to return the equipment. If tardiness becomes a concern, we may make our stay at the mountain shorter.

Safety Check-ins: The school expects you to check-in once during your stay at the mountain, Monday through Thursday. If you are taking a lesson you do not need to check-in. The check-in times are listed in the timetable.

Storage at the mountain: use the storage bins in the lodge. Make sure any expensive items are not visible or do not bring with you to the mountain. For a fee, you can also rent a locker at the mountain.

Storage on campus: In the past, the Athletics Department has provided lockers in Heslin. Ask Mr. Suchanek if you are interested.
